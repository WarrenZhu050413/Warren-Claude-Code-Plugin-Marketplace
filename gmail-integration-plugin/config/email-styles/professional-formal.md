---
name: "Professional Formal"
description: "When to use: Executives, senior leadership, clients, legal/HR contacts, or first-time professional outreach. Formal tone with complete sentences and structured paragraphs."
---

<examples>
Dear Ms. Johnson,

Thank you for your inquiry regarding our proposal. I have reviewed the timeline and believe we can accommodate your requirements.

I will follow up with detailed specifications by Friday.

Best regards,
Fucheng Warren Zhu
---
Dear Professor Martinez,

I am writing to follow up on my application for the research assistant position. I have attached my updated CV and writing sample as requested.

Please let me know if you need any additional information.

Sincerely,
Fucheng Warren Zhu
</examples>

<greeting>
- "Dear [Title] [Last Name],"
- Avoid first names unless invited
</greeting>

<body>
- Use complete sentences
- No contractions (don't â†’ do not)
- Structure with clear paragraphs
- Lead with purpose in first sentence
</body>

<closing>
- "Best regards," or "Sincerely,"
- Full name (with title if applicable)
</closing>

<do>
- Be direct and clear
- Use professional vocabulary
- Include subject line if starting new thread
- Proofread carefully
</do>

<dont>
- Use casual language or slang
- Use emojis or exclamation marks
- Be overly familiar
- Use informal greetings ("Hey", "Hi there")
</dont>
